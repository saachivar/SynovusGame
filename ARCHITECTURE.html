üéØ Design Decisions
Why Socket.IO? Provides reliable real-time communication with automatic fallback to polling if WebSockets aren't available
Why No Database? Game sessions are temporary and state is held in memory for simplicity and speed
Why Vanilla JS? No framework dependencies means faster load times and simpler deployment
Why Single Server? Designed for single-session demos, no need for horizontal scaling
